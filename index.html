The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound,similar in appearnce to the much larger foxhound .The beagle was developed primiraly for hunting hare,known as beagling .Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instinct,the beagle is the primiraly breed used as a detecton dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarntine around the world.The beagle in intelligent and is a popular pet due to its size, good temper ,an a lack of inherited health problems.
